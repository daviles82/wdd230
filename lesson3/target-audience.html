Target Audience

This website is designed to serve the needs of local residents and business owners. Whether you're looking for information about local businesses, community events, or public resources, you'll find it all here. We also offer a variety of tools and resources to help you participate and contribute to your community. So whether you're new to the area or you've been here for years, we encourage you to explore our website and see what we have to offer.

Personas

Ashley

Ashley is the owner of a pet grooming salon in Brookshire, Texas. She is a valued member of the community and a strong advocate for local businesses. Through her participation in the Brookshire Chamber of Commerce, she has helped to ensure that the town remains a competitive player in the Houston area.

Little Kevin

Nine-year-old Kevin is a lemonade stand entrepreneur with a big heart. He has partnered with the Brookshire Chamber of Commerce to spread his business and the presence of other local businesses. Kevin's lemonade stand is a hit, drawing in customers from all over town. He also generates enough traffic to help other businesses spread their reach when he is invited to set up shop at their locations.
